ITZY

Itzy is a South Korean girl group formed by JYP Entertainment and consisting of members: Yeji, Lia, Ryujin, Chaeryeong, and Yuna. They debuted on February 12, 2019. Hwang Ye-ji known as Yeji is a South korean singer. She is the oldest and the leader of the group. Yeji was born on May 26, 2000, in Wansan-gu, Jeonju-si, South Korea. Choi Jisu kown as Lia is a South korean vocalist. She was born on July 21, 2000, in Bucheon-si, South Korea. Shin Ryu Jin known as Ryujin is a South Korean rapper.She was born on April 17, 2001, in Chuncheon-si, South Korea. Lee Chae Ryeong known asa Chaeryrong is a South Korean Singer and Dancer. She was born on June 5, 2001, Yongin-si, South Korea. Shin Yuna known as Yuna is a South Korean Rapper. She was born on December 9, 2003, in Wonju-si, South Korea.
